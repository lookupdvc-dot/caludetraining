Our Story

Advisors you can trust with what you've worked to build

Sterling & Vale Advisory was founded on a single conviction: every family deserves the kind of independent, comprehensive financial counsel that was once reserved for institutions. We are fee-only fiduciaries — our advice is never shaped by commissions or product incentives.

Our team brings together decades of experience across private wealth management, institutional portfolio strategy, and tax-efficient estate planning. We work with a deliberately limited number of client families so that every relationship receives the depth of attention it deserves.

Whether you are building wealth, approaching retirement, or planning for the generation after you — we begin where you are and design a strategy shaped entirely around your life.
